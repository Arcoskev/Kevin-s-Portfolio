About Me

I’m a creative who enjoys designing within the realm of the ever-evolving digital media. What guides me? Positivity, empowerment, diversity, awareness, and adaptability. Proud of my diverse cultural upbringing, I see design as a way to reach those that haven’t been reached. I can adapt to any work environment, with or without a team, on various platforms. Having a background in music production and multimedia, my skills enable me to reach an audience in many formats. At my core, I’m a firm believer in good energy as the root of prosperity.
